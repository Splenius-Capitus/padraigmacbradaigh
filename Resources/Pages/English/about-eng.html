Pádraig Mac Brádaigh

Oifigeach na Gaeilge - Coláiste na Tríonóide

My name is Pádraig Mac Brádaigh and I am a student at Trinity College. I am in my fourth year studying Early and Modern Irish. I live my whole life through the medium of Irish and I am extremely committed to its promotion in all aspects of Irish society. I have just been elected as the first full-time Irish Language Officer in the Trinity College Student Union (AMLCT/TCDSU) and I am looking forward to strengthening the presence of Irish in the College next year.
